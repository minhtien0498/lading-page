Current Research Projects

Smart Water Quality Monitoring System

Developing an IoT-based system for real-time water quality monitoring in urban areas. This project focuses on:

  • Integration of sensors for pH, dissolved oxygen, and contaminants
  • Data collection and analysis platform
  • Early warning system for water quality issues
  • Cost-effective solution for developing countries
In Progress Funded by HCMUS Research Grant

Green Chemistry in Water Treatment

Investigating sustainable methods for water treatment using natural materials. Key aspects include:

  • Development of bio-based adsorbents
  • Optimization of treatment processes
  • Reduction of chemical usage
  • Cost and energy efficiency analysis
In Progress Collaboration with Industry Partners

Advanced Materials for Water Filtration

Research on novel materials for water filtration systems. Project highlights:

  • Development of graphene-based filters
  • Testing with various water sources
  • Long-term performance analysis
  • Cost optimization studies
In Progress National Research Foundation Grant
