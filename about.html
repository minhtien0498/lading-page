About Chemistry Center

Our Mission

Chemistry Center at Ho Chi Minh City University of Science (HCMUS) is dedicated to advancing research in chemistry and environmental sciences. We focus on developing innovative solutions for water quality and environmental protection.

Our Vision

To be a leading research center in Southeast Asia for chemistry and environmental sciences, contributing to sustainable development and environmental protection.
